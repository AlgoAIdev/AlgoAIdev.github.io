About Us

Research-Driven AI Engineering

AlgoAI brings together PhD-level expertise in Natural Language Processing, Speech Analysis, and Machine Learning with hands-on industry experience building production AI systems.

Our work spans the full spectrum from fundamental research to deployed solutions. We've published peer-reviewed research on hedge detection, uncertainty in text, multimodal classification, influence prediction, and information extraction.

We've also built enterprise-grade conversational AI systems that handle complex domain-specific tasks in regulated industries. This combination of academic depth and practical engineering allows us to tackle problems that require both theoretical rigor and real-world robustness.

Based in New York City
