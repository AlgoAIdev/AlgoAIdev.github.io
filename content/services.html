  • Custom NLP models for domain-specific language
  • Evaluation frameworks and ranking systems

Speech & Multimodal AI

We combine speech recognition with rich metadata analysis to extract insights that go beyond transcription. Our multimodal approaches fuse acoustic features, linguistic content, and contextual signals for tasks like speaker profiling and demographic classification.
